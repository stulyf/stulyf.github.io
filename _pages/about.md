Hi, I'm Yu-Feng Li (李昱锋), an undergraduate student majoring in Data Science and Big Data Technology at [South China University of Technology](https://www.scut.edu.cn/) (GPA: 3.77/4.0).

My research interests lie in **deep learning**, **reinforcement learning**, and **large language model applications**. I am currently working as a research intern at SCUT, focusing on multi-agent reinforcement learning and LLM fine-tuning.

I have experience in LLM fine-tuning (LoRA, SFT, DPO) and have participated in competitions like the [AI Mathematical Olympiad](https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/ai-mathematical-olympiad-progress-prize-2) on Kaggle (Silver Medal, ranked 78/2214). My first-author paper _MARGA_ on multi-agent path planning is currently under review at IEEE T-ASE.

You can find more about my projects on [GitHub](https://github.com/stulyf) and [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/crazyzzz7).
